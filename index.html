Welcome to my home page!

I like to consider myself as a curious developer. I love to try new technologies and play with old ones.

Tutorials

When I have to quickly learn about a new tool, I like to take some time afterwards to review the basics and to follow the best practices. This was the case for PHP and Hibernate.

Workshops

A good way to find out about new trends is to get yourself exposed to workshops. From one of them I got familiar with Selenium patterns. And I recently discovered how Java 8 successfuly managed to keep this twenty years old platform up to date!

Training

When a technology seems very different to what you have been used to, it may be appropriate to attend a dedicated training. Le Wagon (a startup giving bootcamp-like training) offered me the great opportunity to learn Ruby on Rails and take-offs at the same time.

Projects

More recently I have been working on some real-life projects. A (French) web application to rank your favorite movies of the year. And the tool chain for an upcoming digital version of a French quiz.
